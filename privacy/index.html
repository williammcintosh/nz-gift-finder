Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information NZ Gift Finder may collect, how it may be used, and the limited situations in which it may be shared.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information you voluntarily submit through the contact form, such as your name, email address, and message. We may also receive basic technical information automatically through your browser or hosting tools, such as IP address, browser type, referring pages, and general site usage data.

2. How we use information

We may use submitted or automatically collected information to:

  • respond to messages and support requests
  • improve the website and user experience
  • understand which pages and gift categories are useful to visitors
  • maintain site security and prevent abuse
3. Affiliate links and third parties

NZ Gift Finder includes affiliate links to third-party retailers, including Amazon. When you click those links, third-party websites may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control the privacy practices of those external websites.

4. Contact form processing

Messages submitted through our contact form may be processed by third-party form handling services so they can be delivered to us. By using the form, you understand that your submission may pass through those service providers.

5. Cookies and analytics

The site may use basic cookies, analytics tools, or hosting logs to help us understand traffic and improve the website. If analytics or advertising tools are added later, this policy may be updated to reflect that.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information when necessary with service providers who help operate the website, process messages, host the site, or comply with legal obligations.

7. Data retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the site, respond to enquiries, maintain records, and meet legal or security needs.

8. Your choices

If you have contacted us and want your information updated or deleted where reasonably possible, email us at contact@nzgiftfinder.com.

9. Children's privacy

NZ Gift Finder is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated version in effect once published.
